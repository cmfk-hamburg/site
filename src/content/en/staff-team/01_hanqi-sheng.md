Hanqi Sheng was born in Hangzhou, China. In 2016, she completed her master's degree in Film and Television at the China Academy of Fine Arts. During her study, she was granted the Art School Alliance Scholarship. From 2016 to 2021, she studied documentary filmmaking with Prof. Pepe Danquart at University of Fine Art Hamburg (HFBK Hamburg). Her main focus and interests are urban culture and feminism topics.
